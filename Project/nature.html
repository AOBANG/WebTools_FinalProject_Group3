Point Lobos State Natural Reserve

62 CA-1, Carmel-By-The-Sea, CA 93923

Point Lobos is the perfect place to get an all in one experience when you're looking for a nature hike. You get the best views, best hiking trails, and best weather! All in one spot that will want you coming back to the amazing trails with amazing views!

Monterey Bay Aquarium

886 Cannery Row, Monterey, CA 93940

The Monterey Bay Aquarium is a spot you cannot miss if you're ever visiting the area! It is a must see, focusing on the marine habitats right here in Monterey Bay. Visiting the aquarium is not only an educational experience but an experience that will change the way you view our oceans.

Lovers Point Park

631 Ocean View Blvd, Pacific Grove, CA 93950

Lovers point park is also a spot you cannot miss. You can go for a walk alongside the ocean with the trails provided or if you're looking to dip your toes in the water you can walk down to the little beach that is open to the public and here you can see some of the clearest water in the bay!
