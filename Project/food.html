Revival Ice Cream

463 Alvarado St, Monterey, CA 93940

Looking for dessert? We got you! Our favorite local ice cream spot is located in Old Monterey, it is called Revival Ice Cream. They are known for only using organic and in-season ingredients! The local favorite is ordering the Bees Knees made with local honey and beeswax. They also have a variety of vegan options as well!

Paris Bakery Cafe

271 Bonifacio Pl, Monterey, CA 93940

If you’re ever craving some french pastries or are just looking for a quick bite head on over to Paris Bakery Cafe. This locally owned business has been serving fresh pastries and food since 1993. You can stop by and grab a pain au chocolat, or if you're feeling a bit more hungry they have delicious sandwiches served on fresh bread of your choice!

Phil's Fish Market & Eatery

7600 Sandholdt Rd, Moss Landing, CA 95039

Located in the heart of Moss Landing, Phil’s Fish Market is a locally owned family business. It is a bit of a drive from Monterey but this place is totally worth it if you're a seafood lover! It is so good they were named number #1 Best Beach restaurant by BBC Travel, and even been featured on Food Network.

Red House Cafe

662 Lighthouse Ave, Pacific Grove, CA 93950

If you're looking for the perfect place to have breakfast or brunch, look no further because the red house cafe is the perfect spot to have your favorite breakfast dish. Located in Pacific Grove, the Red House Cafe has one of the best eggs benedict.
