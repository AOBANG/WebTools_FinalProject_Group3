Del Monte Shopping Center

1410 Del Monte Center, Monterey, CA 93940

Del Monte shopping center is a great spot if you're ready to do some serious shopping. They have a good variety of stores ranging from makeup, clothing, shoes, furniture and food, they have it all! And If you're ready to take a break in between shopping you can grab a bite and sit by the fountain before your next shopping spree.

EdgeWater Shopping Center

Playa Ave, Sand City, CA 93955

Edgewater Shopping Center is perfect for just about any shopping you're trying to get done. There are a variety of grocery, convenience, furniture and clothing stores. IT is the perfect shopping center if you're running errands because you can run all your errands in one spot!

Carmel Plaza

Mission St, Carmel-By-The-Sea, CA 93921

If you are looking to do some shopping that is more on the high-end side, I recommend shopping at carmel plaza. Carmel plaza has a variety of upscale stores that might have what you're looking for if Del Monte Shopping center doesn't! They also have a variety of food options in case you get hungry in between shopping.
