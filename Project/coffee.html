Captain + Stoker

398 E Franklin St, Monterey, CA 93940

Up first on the coffee roster is Captain + Stoker this hipster coffee shop opened up in 2018 and has been thriving since then. They have a good selection of coffee, teas and food, as well as an amazing atmosphere to have a cup of coffee. I would recommend this spot if you're hanging with friends and want to chill at a nice coffee shop!

Alta Bakery and Cafe

502 Munras Ave, Monterey, CA 93940

Alta bakery Cafe is located in Old Town Monterey and also has a great atmosphere to hang out or do some studying! The building this coffee shop is in is a historic building from 1827! They have a simple coffee menu but it does not disappoint, they also have a wide variety of food options to pair with your cup of coffee.

Tidal Coffee

400 Cannery Row, Monterey, CA 93940

If you're looking for a cup of coffee with a beach side view then Tidal Coffee is the place for you! They have a variety of coffee drinks that come with a view of Monterey Bay. The Iced Mocha is a local favorite from there and if you’re hungry don’t worry, they also have a selection of small pastries to go with your coffee.

Power Plant Coffee

7990 CA-1, Moss Landing, CA 95039

Calling all plant lovers and coffee lovers!! This spot is a gem if you're a fan of plants and coffee! They have a great selection of food, coffee, tea, and plants! You can go have a cup of coffee and browse around the shop for some plants.
